How it works

Three steps. Zero excuses.

Most tools let you collect tasks forever. LaterSlayer forces a decision upfront.

1

2

The psychology

Your to-do list is a wishlist in disguise.

Research in behavioural psychology shows that tasks without a committed time are almost never completed. They exist in your list as comfort, not intention.

LaterSlayer removes the escape hatch. Every task demands one question: when, exactly, will you do this? That single friction point separates productive people from perpetual planners.

It's not about motivation. It's about commitment mechanics.
